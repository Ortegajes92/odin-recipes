Huevos Rancheros

Description

Huevos Rancheros are a really quick and easy mexican dish to make for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 4 Tortillas
  • 4 Eggs
  • About 1/2 cup of refried pinto beans
  • some mince garlic as garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan heat oil to approximately 300 degrees.
  2. Drop tortillas one by one in oil till crunchy. Once ready spread the beans on the tortillas.
  3. In the same oil cook eggs in prefered manner, put cooked eggs on top of of tortillas and finish by garnishing with some cilantro, and enjoy.
