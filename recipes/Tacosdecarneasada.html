Carne Asada Tacos

Description

No matter where you are from you have probably have heard of this mexican dish. If you havent figured it out by now im talking about tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Carne para asar
  • cinnamon
  • Salt
  • oregano
  • garlic clove
  • Garlic salt
  • Tortillas

Instructions

  1. season the meat with a pinch of cinnammon, salt, oregano, a minced garlic clove, and garlic salt
  2. Cook carne asada in oil till cooked well, take out and dice the meat
  3. Heat up tortilla and put meat in them, garnish wiht codimnents of your choice
