Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Chicken Enchiladas are a staple in mexican cuisine. This recipe is a recipe passed down my abuela it's a very simple recipe to make too.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups prepare salsa
  • 2 cups Andre and Everett Shredded Mexican Mix Cheese
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 pickled jalapeno peppers minced
  • 2 tsp taco seasoning
  • 8 corn tortillas warmed
  • 1 ripe avocado, peeled pitted and diced
  • 2 tbsp finely chpped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahreinheit. Grease 8-inch square baking dish. Spread 1/2 cup salsa in bottom of dish.
  2. Toss together 1/2 cup cheese, chicken, jalapenos and taco seasoning; divide mixture among tortillas. Roll up tightly and place seam side down in prepared baking dish; top with the remaining salsa and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  3. Bake, uncovered, for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown and bubbling. Top with avocado and cilantro. Drizzle with sour cream and serve with lime wedges.
